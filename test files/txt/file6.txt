 Scopula fuscata is a moth of the Geometridae family. It is found from south-western Saskatchewan west to British Columbia and south to California and Arizona. The habitat consists of montane areas including foothills.The wingspan is 24-28 mm. The wings and body are light tan sprinkled with darker yellow-brown or grey-brown scales. There is one generation per year with adults on wing in late June and early July in the northern part of the range.
